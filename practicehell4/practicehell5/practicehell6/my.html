Zindagi ek kitaab hai, har din ek naya panna.
Kabhi khushi, kabhi gham, lekin har ehsaas apna.
Socho mat zyada, bas jee lo har lamha.
Kal kisne dekha hai, aaj se hi sapna bun lo.
Har girne ke baad, uthna hi toh jeet hai.

Coding bhi ek kala hai, sirf syntax nahi jazbaat hai.
Har semicolon ke peechhe ek kahani chhupi hoti hai.
Jab code chale bina error ke, dil garden ho jaata hai.
Bugs milte hain toh kya, fix karke hi toh maza aata hai.
Console.log karte jao, duniya ko samajhte jao.

Sapne dekho, lekin neend se nahi haq se.
Mehnat se likho apna kal, taqdeer ka kya bharosa.
Har muskil ek moka hai — grow karne ka, nikhharne ka.
Kal ka coder aaj keyboard pe likh raha hai taareekh.
Anuj jaisa banda kabhi haar nahi maanta!
